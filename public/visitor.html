FREQUENTLY ASKED

What are the museum opening hours?

We are open Monday to Friday from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, and Saturday from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays.

Is there an admission fee?

General admission is free for all visitors. Special exhibits and guided tours may have additional fees.

Are group tours available?

Yes! We offer guided group tours for schools, organizations, and private groups. Please contact us to book.

Is the museum accessible?

Yes, our museum is fully wheelchair accessible with ramps, elevators, and accessible restrooms.
